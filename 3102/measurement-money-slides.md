# Money


---

## Defining Characteristics of Money

- Medium of exchange
- Store of value
- Unit of account

---

## Forms of Money 

- Commodity money
- Representations of that commodity:
    - Circulating private notes
    - commodity-backed paper currency
- Fiat currency
- Transactions Deposits
    - Checks, savings, digital currency...


---
